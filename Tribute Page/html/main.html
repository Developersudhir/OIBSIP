Nationality Indian

About He

Sushant Singh Rajput was an impressive actor. Right from his debut film Kai Po Che! to Chhichhore, the actor gave some impreccable performances. The man was passionate about his work and loved watching films.

Sushant Singh Rajput was good at studies and cleared 11 engineering entrance examinations in his entire life.

Education

Sushant, a National Physics Olympiad champion and a seventh-rank holder in the All India Engineering Entrance Examination (AIEEE), used to gaze closely through a telescope at celestial bodies.

He did his schooling from St. Karen's High School in Patna and then from Kulachi Hansraj Model School in New Delhi.

In 2003, he secured the seventh rank in the Delhi College of Engineering Entrance Exam and got admitted in B.E. Mechanical Engineering. Sushant was also a National Olympiad Winner in Physics. At Delhi College of Engineering.

He Actively Participated In Theatre. He Drops Out Of College To Pursue His Acting Career.
